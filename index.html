  • Intro

    Ming Yang is a postdoctoral research fellow in the Geomechanical Modeling Group at Northwestern University starting in September 2021. Before that, he was a postdoctoral scholar in the Theoretical & Applied Geomechancis (TAG) research group at the University of British Columbia (UBC). He obtained the Ph.D. degree in Civil Engineering at UBC in November 2020. Before that, he received the M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees from the Department of Hydraulic Engineering at Tsinghua University in 2015 and 2013, respectively.

    Ming Yang's research interests include physics and mechanics of granular materials, constitutive modeling of geomaterials and applications, and numerical methods in geomechanics.
